# Claim Tagging Schema

## REPO FACT

Observed directly in repository files, code, configuration, tests, or local commands.

## TESTED

Confirmed by a test, command, fixture, smoke run, or reproducible check.

## FIELD DATA

Observed from users, interviews, actual usage, real messages, recorded behavior, or post interview feedback.

## PAYMENT EVIDENCE

Checkout, invoice, Stripe, bank transfer, paid pilot, refund, refusal with price, or other money behavior.

## EXTERNAL SOURCE

Reliable external source. Must cite source, date, and what it supports.

## DOC CLAIM

Claim found in a document, prior strategy note, application draft, AI report, or handoff. It is not truth until verified.

## STRONG INFERENCE

Not directly proven, but supported by several independent signals.

## HYPOTHESIS

Plausible, important, and testable.

## MODEL INFERENCE

Inference made by an AI model based on provided context.

## STORY RISK

A statement that sounds good but could mislead a founder, investor, accelerator, customer, or Codex.

## NO EVIDENCE

No supporting evidence found.
