# NAILIT Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-11

## What we collect
- Email address (to send your prep pack and follow up)
- CV and job description (to generate your prep pack)
- Company and role name (to personalise research)
- Session activity (to improve the product)

## What we do NOT store
- Your raw CV after your prep pack is generated
- Any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above

## How long we keep it
- Session artifacts: 7 days after creation
- Session records: 90 days after last activity
- You can request deletion at any time

## Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)
- Access: GET /user/{your_id}/data-export
- Deletion: DELETE /user/{your_id}/data
- Questions: privacy@nailit.ai

## Data processors
- Anthropic (AI inference) — anthropic.com/privacy
- Vercel (hosting) — vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy
- Supabase (database) — supabase.com/privacy
- Stripe (payments) — stripe.com/privacy
